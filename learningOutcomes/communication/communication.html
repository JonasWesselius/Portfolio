Teams

Microsoft Teams was an essential communication tool used throughout my project, both for internal coordination within my project group and for interactions with teachers. It significantly enhanced my ability to collaborate effectively and stay organized.

Within our project group, we used Microsoft Teams to facilitate discussions, share documents, and provide updates. The platform's group chat and channel features allowed us to maintain organized conversations and easily access shared resources. This ensured that all team members were well-informed and could contribute effectively to the project's progress.

For interactions with teachers, Microsoft Teams served as a platform for scheduling meetings, receiving feedback, and clarifying doubts. I could easily arrange virtual consultations, share my progress, and receive immediate feedback from our teachers. This ongoing interaction helped me stay aligned with the project requirements and make necessary adjustments based on the guidance received.

Overall, Microsoft Teams played a pivotal role in streamlining my communication and enhancing the efficiency of my project workflow, ensuring smooth collaboration and continuous support from my teachers.

Meetings

As part of my project, I scheduled an online meeting with my client, Lighttown Speakers, to conduct a detailed interview. This meeting aimed to understand the project requirements more deeply and to get to know my client better. Using Microsoft Teams, we arranged a convenient time for both parties, ensuring that all key members were available for the discussion.

During the interview, I asked insightful questions to gather comprehensive information about their needs, expectations, and preferences. This conversation allowed me to clarify the project scope, identify critical areas for improvement, and align my design approach with the client's vision. The insights gained from this meeting were invaluable in guiding my project direction and ensuring that my solutions were tailored to meet the specific needs of Lighttown Speakers.

Visit

Our team visited Lighttown Speakers to gain a deeper understanding of the club and its members. During the visit, we conducted an affinity mapping session to gather insights directly from the club members. This hands-on activity allowed us to learn about the members' needs, preferences, and areas where the club's website could be improved.

We had the opportunity to observe a club meeting in action, which gave us a unique inside look at how Lighttown Speakers operates. We saw firsthand the structure of their meetings, the roles members take on, and the activities they participate in. This experience provided valuable context for our project and helped us align our design solutions with the actual practices and goals of the club.

Additionally, we witnessed a member graduate from their pathway, which highlighted the club's supportive and goal-oriented environment. This visit was instrumental in shaping our understanding of the client and ensuring our project meets the real needs of Lighttown Speakers and its members.
